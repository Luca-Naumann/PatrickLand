Where Joy Comes to Play

PatrickLand is a vibrant family playground and amusement park located at 8 Koorabel Rd, Lalor Park NSW 2147. We create safe, exciting, and unforgettable memories for kids of all ages and their families.

  • Safe & supervised play areas
  • Themed zones for toddlers to big kids
  • Picnic spots, shade sails & café area
